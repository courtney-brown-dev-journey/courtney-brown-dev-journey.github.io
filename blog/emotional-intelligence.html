Emotional Intelligence ❤️

Emotional intelligence is the ability to use, understand and manage your own emotions as well as other people’s. The key components are tied to the following awareness/skillset:
-Acknowledge emotions
-Differentiate and analyse emotions
-Accept and appreciate emotions
-Reflect on your emotions and their origin
-Handle your Emotions
-Handle the Emotions of Others

An IQ test measures your reasoning and problem-solving abilities. However, it’s not necessarily tied in to understanding emotions or how to navigate them. Emotional intelligence is important as it’s tied to creating stronger friendships and connections to others, as understanding is strengthened. It helps you to analyse, adjust and support yourself when you’re getting close to a burn out, or any distress. This also helps you to reduce stress and decrease stress as you’re aware of your own emotions as well as everyone else’s. To strengthen your emotional intelligence is to strengthen your resilience, motivation, empathy, reasoning, stress management, and communication.
